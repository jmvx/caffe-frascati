The Best Capuccino on 3 Wheels!

With its quirky styling and cheeky Italian looks, the iconic Piaggio Ape (“ah-pay”) has been Italy’s favorite utility van for over 60 years. Now fitted out with state-of-the-art espresso machines, they are a common sight at farmers markets and events across Europe.

Caffe Frascati has what we believe to be the only one in the USA, and we will be thrilled to bring it to your next event and make your guests the best espresso drinks they’ve ever tasted!
